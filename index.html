Student Satisfaction Survey

University of Foreign Language Studies

This survey will collect data of a student's satisfaction at the university in several particular fields. It contains questions that collect data about the referenceability of the school, grading or rating questions about school services and teaching quality as well as feedback about academic instructor, school staff and school services.
